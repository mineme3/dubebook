
"Act as an expert UI/UX Designer. Create a complete mobile app design system and screen flow in Figma for 'DubeNote', an offline-first credit tracking app for informal shop owners. 

**1. Design Tokens & Variables:**
- **Colors**: 
  - Background: #FFFFFF
  - Surface: #F8FAFC
  - Primary: #1010A0 (Electric Blue)
  - Accent/Success: #059669 (Green)
  - Text Primary: #0F172A
  - Text Secondary: #64748B
  - Error: #DC2626
  - Input Fill: #F1F5F9
- **Typography**: Roboto/Inter. 
  - Huge: 48px, Black (900)
  - Titles: 20px, Black (900), 3px letter spacing.
  - Body: 16px, Medium (500).
- **Corner Radii**: Buttons/Inputs = 16px, Cards = 24px, Hero Cards = 30px.

**2. Component Library Requirements:**
- **Buttons**: Elevated (Solid Primary), Outlined (Primary border, transparent bg). Include Idle, Pressed, and Disabled states.
- **Inputs**: Filled grey background, no border. Focused state adds a 2px Primary Blue border.
- **List Items**: Customer Tile with an avatar box, Name, Due Date, Debt Amount. Include an 'Overdue' variant with red text and red border.
- **Hero Card**: Gradient card displaying Total Outstanding Balance with large typography.

**3. Screen Designs Required (Mobile 390x844):**
- **Splash/Lock Screen**: Centered logo, PIN/Password entry if locked.
- **Dashboard**: AppBar with Settings/History icons. Hero Card at top. List of Unpaid Customers below. Floating Action Button 'New Customer' at bottom right.
- **Add Customer Dialog**: Modal with Name input, and a 'Select Deadline' row.
- **Customer Details Screen**: Hero header showing specific customer total debt. Tabular or list view of individual transactions (items taken vs payments). 'Add Transaction' button.
- **History Screen**: Chronological list of all global transactions.
- **Settings**: Language selector (Amharic, Oromo, English), App Lock toggle, Theme info.

**4. Layout & Grid:**
- 4-column mobile grid.
- Margins: 24px horizontal.
- Gutter: 16px.
- Follow Material 3 spacing guidelines but with more generous border radii for a softer, modern feel."
